Y ou must make haste for you sense it is not safe to linger by the smoking remains of the ruined monastery. The black-winged beasts could return at any moment. You must set out for the Sommlending capital of Holmgard and tell the King the terrible news of the massacre: that the whole elite of Kai warriors save yourself have been slaughtered. Without the Kai Lords to lead her armies Sommerlund will be at the mercy of their ancient enemy the Darklords.

Fighting back tears you bid farewell to your dead kinsmen. Silently you promise that their deaths will be avenged. You turn away from the ruins and carefully descend the steep track.

At the foot of the hill the path splits into two directions both leading into a large wood.
